\title{Total Cross Section}

\> Luminosity Independent Method
\>> based on Optical theorem: $\si_{\rm tot} \propto \Im A(t = 0)$

$$\si_{\rm tot} = {1\over 1+\rh^2} {{\d N/ \d t|_0}\over N_{\rm el} + N_{\rm inel}}, \qquad\qquad {\cal L} = (1+\rh^2)\, {(N_{\rm el} + N_{\rm inel})^2\over {\d N/ \d t|_0}}$$
\vskip\baselineskip

\htab{\bln
	&	& \be^* = 90\un{m} & 1535\un{m} \cr\bln
\d N/\d t|_0	& \hbox{Extrapolation of elastic rate to } t = 0 & 4\percent & 0.2 \percent \cr\ln
N_{\rm el}	& \hbox{Total elastic rate (correlated with extrapolation)} & 2\percent & 0.1\percent \cr\ln
N_{\rm inel}	& \vbox{\hsize7cm\noindent\strut Total inelastic rate (error dominated by Single Diffractive trigger losses)} & 1\percent & 0.8\percent \cr\ln
\rh\equiv\Re A(t)/\Im A(t)|_{t = 0} & \vcenter{\hsize7cm\noindent\strut Error contribution from $(1 + \rh^2)$ (using full COMPETE error band)\phantom{g}} &\multispan2\vrule\hfil $1.2\percent$\hfil \cr\bln
&\hbox{Total for } \si_{\rm tot} & 5\percent & 1\div2\percent \cr\bln
&\hbox{Total for } {\cal L} & 7\percent & 2\percent \cr\bln
}

\title{Optics details}
\abovedisplayshortskip=3pt
\belowdisplayshortskip=3pt

\> $\ep$ = emittance ($3.75$ normal, $1$ reduced for low $\be^*$ optics)
\> $\gamma = E/m_0$ relativistic factor of proton

\> proton trajectory
$$x(s) = \sqrt{\ep \be(s)} \cos\phi(s) + D(s)\xi$$

\> high $\be^* \Rightarrow$ thick beam $\Rightarrow$ need for parallel-to-point focusing $\equiv$ negligible $v$-terms

\> optical functions
$$v(s) = \sqrt{\be(s)\over\be^*} \cos\phi(s)$$
$$L(s) = \sqrt{\be(s)\be^*} \sin\phi(s)$$
$$\phi(s) = \int_0^s {\d s'\over\be(s')}$$

$$\sigma(\hbox{vertex}) = \sqrt{\ep \be^*\over \gamma}$$
$$\sigma(\hbox{beam divergence}) = \sqrt{\ep \over \be^* \gamma}$$

\> details: see \Bref{hubert thesis}
